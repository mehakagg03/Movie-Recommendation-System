MOVIE RECOMMENDATION SYSTEM
PROJECT

A Movie Recommendation System is an algorithm that suggests movies to users based on their preferences, past behavior and also on the features or attributes of the movies themselves, such as cast, director, and other keywords. The system analyzes user interactions (like ratings, views, or genres) and compares them to similar users or movies to make personalized recommendations.
